About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University. I am a part of WINGS lab and advised by Prof. Samir Das. I broadly work in the areas of edge computing and passive backscatter networks.

Research Interests

  • Edge and mobile computing: Optimizing video analytics applications’ performance and resource management in resource-starved edge devices.
  • Passive backscatter networks: (i) Collaborative backscatter schemes to increase the range of passive backscatter tags and (ii) Energy-aware protocols for applications on passive backscatter devices (eg. localization).
